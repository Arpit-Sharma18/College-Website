ABOUT MRIEM

Matu Ram Institute of Engineering and Management (MRIEM) is a private college in Rohtak, Haryana, India. It was established in 2008 and is affiliated with Maharshi Dayanand University.

DIRECTOR'S CORNER

Dr. Navneet Ahlawat is the Director of the Matu Ram Institute of Engineering and Management (MRIEM) in Rohtak, Haryana. Ahlawat has a PhD and 20 years of experience.
